CAPSTONE PROJECT

In this project, I utilized Power BI to analyze sales data, focusing on key performance indicators to drive business insights. The visualizations created include a detailed breakdown of profits by segment, which highlights the most lucrative areas of the business. I also identified the top 10 sales by city, providing a geographical perspective on performance, and ranked the top 10 states based on profit, allowing for targeted strategic decisions. Additionally, I visualized sales categorized by product type, enabling a deeper understanding of market trends. The project features two interactive slicers for category and segment, allowing users to filter data dynamically and gain tailored insights into the sales landscape.
